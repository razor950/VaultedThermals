Detailed Description

Specification

A queue is allocated by the family, as in each queue allocated must have their index in the family tracked. This means that the logical device is passed a queue's create info by the family with its specified index, queue count from that family to be associated with the logical device, and the family's priority.

If a queue family is to be used for a specific task (transfer, etc), it is advantageous that that queue family have as little flags for other functionality as possible.
